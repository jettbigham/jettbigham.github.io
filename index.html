History

Jett’s Cat Rescue was started in 2011 when a stray kitten was found and needed a loving home. The kitten was only a few weeks old and all alone. She was found by a little girl who decided to name her Cutie Pie. The little girl could not believe that Cutie Pie had no one to look after her and decided to keep an eye out for all strays. Cutie Pie was raised by the little girl. Since then, every stray cat has been taken in, cared for, and found a new home.

Adoption Information

Each stray cat brought into the cat rescue program is seen by a veterinarian and evaluated. Any issues or concerns are treated. Once the cat is well, it is available for rehoming. Potential homes are evaluated before any cat is adopted. Some cats may have special conditions that need to be meet before the adoption is final. All adoptions will include up-to-date vaccinations, 1-month check-ups, and food for a week.
